Current Research Focus

I am currently developing Physamp, an assistive technology designed to bridge the communication gap for deaf and mute individuals through intelligent sign-to-speech translation. The system captures hand gestures via a camera-based input pipeline, interprets them using CNN with sequence modeling (LSTM/Transformer), and converts the output into natural speech through a Text-to-Speech (TTS) engine. My research focuses on real-time processing, model optimization, and gesture accuracy to build a scalable, accessible, and impactful solution.
